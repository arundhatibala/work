Hi! I'm a rising senior at Ashoka University studying Computer Science, while dabbling in English, Psychology, Philosophy and Visual Arts.

I'm interested in the intersection of technology and the humanities, and I'm passionate about data-driven approaches to advance sustainability and accessibility in digital products.

I've been a member of several international academies, including The New York Academy of Sciences, University of Hong Kong's Academy for the Talented, the National Society of High School Scholars and the WICCI India-US Business Council.

I'm also a founding member of my university's Centre for Analysis of Networks Data and Insight Derivation (CANDID), where I use sentiment, network and frequency analysis to understand the dissemination of information in social networks.

I'm currently applying to graduate schools for a Master's degree in Human-Computer Interaction and Digital Humanities for the Fall 2023 intake.

When I'm not working, I'm probably drawing, reading, or playing the piano.
